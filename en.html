Alarm and keys

The alarm system protects the apartment and its contents. Please activate it every time you leave the apartment unattended. Before activating it, make sure all windows are closed.

1. Close the doorLeave the apartment and close the front door properly.
2. Wake up the keypadPress any button to activate the keypad.
3. Use the NFC tagHold the NFC tag attached to the keys near the upper area above the numbers.
4. Arm or disarmPress 9 to activate the alarm. Press 10 to deactivate it. When activated, the door automatically locks.

Keypad lights: light 1 = alarm activated; light 2 = system deactivated.

Keys: we will leave a set of keys at the apartment entrance. If we are not present at check-in, we will open the door remotely. At check-out, you can leave the keys in the entrance container or on the living room table; once outside, enter the code or message us so we can activate the alarm remotely.

Zuki will stay with you

Zuki is our Maine Coon, very sociable and affectionate. During your stay he will remain at home with you: thank you very much for taking care of him.

WaterChange his water once a day.
FoodGive him dry food once a day.
Litter boxFor stays longer than 2 days, please clean the litter box at least every 2 days.
Important: please do not let him access the street-side balcony. That balcony has no protections and Zuki tends to climb on the handrail.
Tables and fragile objects▾
Zuki is curious and enjoys knocking things down. Please do not leave glasses, cups, open bottles or fragile objects on the table.
Night routine▾
At night we usually keep him in the bathroom, where he has access to the balcony. He is used to it and we do this to avoid being woken up by him. You are of course free to manage him as you prefer.
